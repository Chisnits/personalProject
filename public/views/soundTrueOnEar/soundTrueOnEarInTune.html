In tune and in touch


SoundTrueTM headphones are designed specifically for use with your

iPhone and other Apple devices. The integrated inline remote, located

on the back of the microphone, lets you control music

functions-adjust volume, play/pause tracks, skip tracks

forward/backward, fast forward/rewind and change playlists. You can

also switch between calls and music with one-touch ease-all without

reaching for your device.
